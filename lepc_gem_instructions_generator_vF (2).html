Instructor Setup Guide
Follow these steps to create the Gem for your class
1
Click Copy above to copy the full Gem instructions to your clipboard
2
Go to gemini.google.com → Explore Gems → New Gem
3
Paste the instructions into the Gem's instruction field. Name it something like "LEPC Town Hall — [Chemical], [City]"
4
No PDF upload needed. Panel members are resolved by the Session Launcher and passed directly in the SESSION CONFIG — the Gem never needs to look anything up
5
Click Save, then share the Gem link with your students
6
Students open the Session Launcher, click Generate, and copy their SESSION CONFIG. They paste it as the first message in the Gem — simulation begins immediately
